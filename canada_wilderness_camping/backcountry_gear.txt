**Essential Backcountry Gear for Wilderness Camping in Canada**

- Tent, sleeping bag, sleeping pad
- Water filter or purification tablets
- Lightweight stove and fuel
- Bear-resistant food container or hang system
- First aid kit, map, compass, GPS
- Extra clothing layers, rain gear
- Emergency whistle, flashlight/headlamp
- Bug spray and sunscreen

Always pack for unpredictable weather and be ready for temperature swings, even in summer.
