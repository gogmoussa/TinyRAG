**Camping in Ontario Provincial Parks**

Ontario has over 100 provincial parks offering backcountry camping. These areas are usually accessible by canoe or long hikes.

- Reservations open 5 months in advance
- Campsites often include fire pits and basic toilets
- You are expected to practice Leave No Trace principles
- Some parks have zone-based permits, others site-specific

Popular parks: Algonquin, Killarney, Quetico, and Lake Superior Provincial Park.
